Coronavirus Tracking for The New York Times

My work with The New York Times is focused on tracking the spread of the coronavirus across the U.S., including at colleges and universities, in nursing homes, and in other coronavirus clusters.

I work with a team of about 25 journalists collecting data from states and counties, public records, and email and phone correspondences with thousands of colleges and facilities.

Over a few months, I have spearheaded an effort to file over a hundred public records requests seeking coronavirus data from public universities. I also helped to create the system that The Times now uses to file and follow up on records requests for coronavirus data at public universities and state health departments.
