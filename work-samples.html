Reporting

My work with The New York Times has been focused on tracking the spread of the coronavirus across the U.S., including at colleges and universities, in nursing homes, and in other coronavirus clusters, and the national rollout of coronavirus vaccines.

I spearheaded an effort to file over a hundred public records requests seeking coronavirus data from public universities. I also helped to create the system that The Times now uses to file and follow up on records requests for coronavirus data at public universities and state health departments.
